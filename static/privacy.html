Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

⚠️ Important Disclaimer: Ledger ERP is an independent application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the Pi Core Team or Pi Network Foundation.

1. Introduction

Ledger ERP ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Non-Custodial ERP System for Pi Network.

2. Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Ledger ERP operates on a zero-knowledge architecture. This means:

  • All sensitive data (encryption keys, mnemonics, PINs) are encrypted client-side before transmission
  • Our backend servers store only encrypted blobs and cannot decrypt your data
  • We never have access to your private keys, wallet addresses, or unencrypted financial data

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Pi Network Authentication: We use Pi.authenticate() to verify your identity. We do not store your Pi username or password.
  • Business Data: Invoice data, customer names, transaction records (all encrypted client-side)
  • Encryption Keys: Stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB (never transmitted unencrypted)

3.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, IP address (for security and rate limiting)
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics (Req #27) - no personally identifiable information
  • Service Worker Data: Cached files for offline functionality

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our ERP services
  • To process Pi Network payments securely
  • To sync encrypted data across your devices (with your explicit consent)
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security
  • To comply with legal obligations (AML reporting - Req #21)

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All sensitive data is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB with persistent storage requests
  • Encryption: AES-GCM encryption with PIN-derived keys (PBKDF2)
  • Backend Storage: Only encrypted blobs (zero-knowledge sync)
  • No Third-Party Storage: We do not use external cloud services for sensitive data

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only:

  • With Pi Network for payment processing (as required by Pi SDK)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To prevent fraud or security threats
  • With your explicit consent (e.g., recovery vault sharing)

7. Your Rights

  • Access: You can export your data at any time (Key Shard Export - Req #12)
  • Deletion: You can delete your local data by clearing browser storage
  • Encryption: You control your encryption keys (non-custodial)
  • Opt-Out: You can disable anonymous telemetry (Req #27)

8. Third-Party Services

  • Pi Network SDK: Subject to Pi Network's privacy policy
  • CDN Services: jsdelivr.net, esm.sh (for JavaScript libraries)
  • No Analytics: We do not use Google Analytics or similar tracking services

9. Children's Privacy

Ledger ERP is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
