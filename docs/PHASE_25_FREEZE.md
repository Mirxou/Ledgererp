# Phase 25 Freeze: Strategic Governance Decision

## Overview
As of February 7, 2026, the **Phase 25 (Production Launch)** of the Pi Ledger ERP system has been **consciously frozen**.

## Rationale
- **Infrastructure Readiness**: The decision is decoupled from technical failure. It is a governance choice based on the current absence of a dedicated production domain and the lack of external pressure for an immediate launch.
- **Sovereign Alignment**: The system must first meet the "Sovereignty" criteria established in Phase 26.
- **Vision 2030**: Shifting focus towards a 10-20 year vision ensures that the platform is built for resilience, adaptability, and long-term viability.

## Impact
- Development moves into **Phase 26: Preparatory Sovereignty** and the **Vision 2030** roadmap.
- The system will prioritize **understanding** and **justice** over launch speed.
- Launch becomes a **consequence** of readiness, not a deadlined goal.

> *"Justice is not showcased; it is delivered when ready."*
