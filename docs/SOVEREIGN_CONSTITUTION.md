# Sovereign Constitution

## Core Philosophy
Pi Ledger is built on the principle that technology should serve justice, not the other way around. This constitution governs all automated and human processes within the system.

## Article I: Principles of Justice
1. **Evidence-Based Judgment**: No judgment or penalty shall be applied without verifiable evidence.
2. **Burden of Proof**: The burden of proof lies with the claimant.
3. **Presumption of Peace**: In cases of doubt, the system interprets data in favor of peace and stability, not conflict.

## Article II: Stewardship (الاستخلاف)
1. **User Ownership**: Users own their data and their digital identity.
2. **System as Tool**: Technology is a tool for human flourishing, not a master of human behavior.
3. **Human Dignity**: Efficiency shall never prioritize system throughput over human dignity.

## Article III: Mercy & Context (عدم الظلم)
1. **No Automated Finality**: Automation assists in analysis but never rendered a final judgment without human review capability.
2. **Contextual Awareness**: The system must allow for nuance and mercy. Speed is secondary to accuracy and fairness.

## Article IV: Accountability (المسؤولية)
1. **Transparent Governance**: Decision-making rules are transparent and auditable.
2. **Community Responsibility**: Those with judicial or administrative privileges are accountable to the community they serve.
3. **Privacy-Preserving Transparency**: System legitimacy is maintained through anonymized public records, never through the exposure of private lives.
